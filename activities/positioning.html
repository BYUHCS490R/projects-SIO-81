Q AND A

What is normal flow in HTML?

Elements just stack one after another from top to bottom.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float makes an element move left or right so text wraps around it. You clear float to stop layout problems.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Relative: Move element a bit but keeps space.
Absolute: Move element anywhere on the page relative to parent.
Fixed: Stays in the same place even when you scroll.
