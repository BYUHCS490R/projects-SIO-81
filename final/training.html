Core exercises and suggested durations for rugby training:

Basic rugby training exercises and suggested durations
Exercise Name Description Duration / Reps
Dynamic Warm-up Leg swings, walking lunges, arm circles 5–10 minutes
Passing Drill Partner passing while moving, focusing on accuracy 10 minutes
Shuttle Runs Short sprints with quick turns 6 × 40 m sprints
Plank Hold Core stability exercise 3 × 45 seconds
