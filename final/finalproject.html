Title: Life is Like Rugby

1. Why I Chose This Topic

Life and rugby feel very similar to me. Rugby has taught me teamwork, discipline, communication, and how to push through hard times. These lessons helped me grow as a player and as a person. I want to show others how rugby can teach life skills, improve fitness, and help people stay mentally strong. This project lets me combine something I love with important life lessons.
